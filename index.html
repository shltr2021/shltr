Making waiting for the bus a little less lonely

The problem

We've all waited for a bus at some point in our lives, and whether you're waiting by yourself for five minutes or 25, it can get a bit lonely.

The solution

We're using transportation to connect people to people rather than people to places, turning the physical bus shelter into a virtual hub of communication. Read about how your fellow commuters are feeling and leave a note of your own in augmented reality.

How it works

    • 1. Scan the ground

    • 2. Tap the marker

    • 3. Write your note

    • 4. Place your note
